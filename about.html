PVSS Associates is an associateship firm between Ranga Construction and CS Design Studio, former focusing on Construction Management and latter focusing on Design & Research. Ranga Construction being the First Architectural firm in Salem District.

On 29th August 1986, the firm was established under the name Ranga Construction, Founded by Ar. P.V.Subramanian, working on Architecture consultancy and turnkey projects. On 27th March 2013, the firm established its design and research wing under the name CS Design Studio, lead by Ar. S.Venugopal.

With more than 40 years of design and execution expertise in the field of Architecture, the firm has been exposed to wider spectrum of Architecture Projects in all major typologies at varied scales.

An architect is a person who plans, designs and reviews the construction of buildings. To practice architecture means to provide services in connection with the design of buildings and the space within the site surrounding the buildings that have human occupancy or use as their principal purpose.[1] Etymologically, architect derives from the Latin architectus, which derives from the Greek (arkhi-, chief + tekton, builder), i.e., chief build

A key part of the design is that the architect often consults with engineers, surveyors and other specialists throughout the design, ensuring that aspects such as the structural supports and air conditioning elements are coordinated in the scheme as a whole. The control and planning of construction costs are also a part of these consultations. Coordination of the different aspects involves a high degree of specialized communication, including advanced computer technology such as BIM (Building Information Modeling), CAD, and cloud-based technologies.
